# Content Strategy: "The Art of Slow Fashion"

## Goal
Drive traffic from "Aesthetic/Lifestyle" interest to [Floral by hand](https://floralbyhand.com/de) by building trust and emotional connection to the manufacturing process.

## Steps
1. **Design & Setup:** Create a blog-focused, personal-voice website.
2. **Storytelling (Fabric & Craft):** Write a feature post about the "leftover fabric" philosophy.
3. **Product Integration:** Curate posts that feature specific shop products as the hero of the lifestyle.
4. **Deploy:** Use GitHub/Vercel (as established previously).

## Blog Post Themes
- **Post 1 (The Philosophy):** "Why we choose leftover fabrics: The beauty of imperfection and the path to sustainable fashion."
- **Post 2 (The Craft):** "From sketch to stitch: How our Vietnamese artisans transform scraps into your favorite coquette pouch."
- **Post 3 (The Style):** "Sunday Morning: A minimalist's guide to packing your essentials."
